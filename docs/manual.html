User Manual for Students and Teachers

This manual provides instructions for students and teachers on how to use the system effectively. It covers logging in, resetting passwords, navigating the student and teacher interfaces, assigning homework, entering grades, recording absences, and logging out.

1. Logging In

Students and Teachers

  1. Go to the login page: [yourwebsite.com/login]
  2. Enter your username and password.
  3. Click Login.
  4. If your credentials are correct:
    • Students will be redirected to their profile page.
    • Teachers will be redirected to the teacher dashboard.

2. Resetting or Changing Password

Forgot Password?

  1. On the login page, click Forgot Password?
  2. Enter your registered email and click Submit.
  3. Check your email for a password reset link.
  4. Follow the instructions to create a new password.

Change Password While Logged In

  1. Go to your profile settings.
  2. Click Change Password.
  3. Enter your current password, then enter and confirm your new password.
  4. Click Save Changes.

3. Student Dashboard

After logging in, students can:

  • View their assigned class and teacher.
  • Check grades for each subject.
  • View assigned homework along with due dates and downloadable attachments.
  • See recorded absences (if applicable).
  • Log out using the logout button.

4. Teacher Dashboard

After logging in, teachers can:

  • View students by class.
  • Assign homework to a class.
  • Enter grades for individual students.
  • Record student absences.
  • Log out using the logout button.

5. Assigning Homework (Teachers)

  1. Navigate to your dashboard.
  2. Select a class where you want to assign homework.
  3. Click Assign Homework.
  4. Enter the title, description, and due date.
  5. (Optional) Attach any necessary files.
  6. Click Submit.

6. Entering Grades (Teachers)

  1. Navigate to the student list.
  2. Click Add Grade next to the student’s name.
  3. Enter grades for each subject.
  4. Click Save.

7. Recording Absences (Teachers)

  1. Navigate to the student list.
  2. Click Add Absence next to the student’s name.
  3. Select the date of the absence.
  4. Click Submit.

8. Logging Out

  1. Click the Logout button in the top menu.
  2. You will be redirected to the homepage.

For further assistance, please contact the system administrator.
